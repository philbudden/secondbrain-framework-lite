## Maintenance Notes

Update this Context Pack when:

- the purpose or scope changes;
- a decision is made;
- an action changes owner, due date, or status;
- a risk appears, changes, or closes;
- a meeting creates durable context that future chats will need.

Do not add routine daily activity here unless it changes the durable understanding of the workstream. Routine activity belongs in the Daily Note.
